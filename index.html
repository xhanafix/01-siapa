Sasaran (Target)

Identifies your ideal customer profile and target market segments. This includes demographics, behaviors, needs, and preferences of your potential customers.

    Isu (Issue)

    Outlines the key problems, pain points, and challenges that your target audience currently faces. These are the issues your product or service aims to solve.

      Akibat (Consequence)

      Describes the negative impacts and risks if the identified issues are not addressed. This helps create urgency and demonstrates the importance of your solution.

        Potensi (Potential)

        Highlights the benefits, opportunities, and positive outcomes that your product or service delivers. Shows how your solution transforms problems into advantages.

          Action (Action)

          Provides clear, actionable steps for your target audience. These are specific actions that lead to engaging with your product or service.
